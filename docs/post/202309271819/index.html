电流定义 Electric Current Definition:

$I= \frac{Q}{t}$

$I$ represent electric current in amperes $(A).$ $Q$ represent electric charge in coulombs $(C).$ $t$ represent time in seconds $(s).$

欧姆定律 Ohm's Law

$I=\frac{V}{R}$

物理: V电压 R电阻 I电流 Intensité(由法国物理数学家安德烈-马里·安培命名) Physics: Voltage Resistance Intensité(intensity, electric current)(the current, named by André-Marie Ampère)
